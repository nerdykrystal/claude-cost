Onondaga County DSS aid programs (specifically ESNA / Emergency Safety Net Assistance, and likely other DSS-administered emergency-cash funds) are **structured as LOANS**. Once a household receives DSS aid through these programs, they are **locked out of further DSS aid until the first loan is repaid in full**.

**Why:** Krystal articulated this 2026-05-13 from lived experience: *"once they give you a loan through their program that's it, if you ever need another loan again you can't get anything until you've paid the first one back; best to exhaust the options."*

**How to apply:** When sequencing applications for crisis assistance (back rent, utilities, food, etc.), prioritize in this order:

1. **First-tier — grants and free assistance** (no future-eligibility cost):
   - Non-profit grants: Catholic Charities (back rent), Salvation Army H.A.L.E. (up to 24mo rent assistance, employment-tied), InterFaith Works of CNY, PEACE Inc.
   - Government GRANT programs: SNAP, HEAP Cooling, HEAP Regular (when in season)
   - Federal grant programs (ERA when active, EHV when accepting) — historically grant-structured

2. **Second-tier — ONLY after first-tier exhausted:**
   - DSS ESNA (loan-structured; consumes future DSS-aid budget until repaid)
   - DSS TA (Temporary Assistance — ongoing cash; separate 5-year lifetime cap)

**Exception — DSS programs that are NOT loans:**
- **SNAP recertification** at DSS office — grant-structured benefit, no loan-lockout
- **HEAP Cooling Component** — grant-structured, no loan-lockout
- **HEAP Regular** (when in season) — grant-structured

Going to DSS for these specifically does NOT trigger the loan-lockout. The lockout is triggered by ESNA/TA cash-aid loans only.

**Practical sequencing example (Krystal's case 2026-05-14):**
- ✅ Apply for SNAP recertification at DSS (she missed recert — this is a re-application)
- ✅ Apply for HEAP Cooling at DSS (needs physician doc for medical-grounds path; she has CPTSD — heat-sensitive vulnerability potentially documentable by her PCP/therapist)
- ❌ Do NOT apply for ESNA at tomorrow's DSS visit — pursue non-profits first; ESNA stays as last-resort fallback if non-profits don't cover the back-rent gap

**Pattern:** Always check before recommending a DSS application — is this aid grant-structured or loan-structured? Loans consume future eligibility; exhaust grants first. Krystal's lived experience is the standpoint authority on this.
