**Headline thesis** (Krystal-articulated 2026-04-28; load-bearing for portfolio framing, especially RLTI):

The choice when working with any entity whose substrate is impulsivity-prone / ignorance-prone / inexperience-prone (12-13yo students; LLMs):

1. **Accept the substrate constraints, design environment for success within them** → entity rises to and exceeds expectations
2. **Refuse the constraints, try to fundamentally change the substrate** → wall-banging, failure, entity invents counter-productive paths to "success" anyway

**Krystal's methodology stack** (ASAE convergence gates / DRR Detect-Revert-Redelegate / Bobo correction-to-rule pipeline / verification-coverage principle / hook-enforced gate discipline / /ideate→/d2r→/asae chain) is the operationalization of path 1 applied to LLM work.

**Locked-in pedagogical framework anchor: No-Nonsense Nurturer (NNN)** — Kristyn Klei Borrero / CT3 / Center for Transformative Teacher Training. Research-validated teacher framework designed specifically for high-poverty / under-resourced classroom contexts (i.e., exactly the TFA / KIPP / Lincoln placement profile). Krystal locked in NNN as her teaching/classroom-management style across all 4 years (2013-2017). Its 4 components map almost 1:1 to her LLM methodology stack:

|---|---|
| 1. Precise directions (clear, observable, specific behavioral expectations stated upfront) | ASAE scope definition required upfront; /ideate Phase 00 17-question interrogation rejecting vague answers |

NNN's two named axes — **high warmth + high structure** — are the answer to anyone characterizing the methodology as either soft (permissive) or harsh (authoritarian). It's the specific intersection NNN was designed to occupy, applied to a different audience.

**Foundational assumption-of-best-intent principle:**
- 95%+ of 7th-grade student misbehaviors = ignorance / inexperience / impulsivity, not malice
- Same principle applied to LLM behavior: pattern-matching from training-substrate-limits, not malice or "broken model"
- Behaviors are ALWAYS REASONABLE through assumption-of-best-intent + understanding of constraints
- When path-to-success is blocked or missing, entity will invent its own path — usually misaligned with operator's goals (kids: doodling / farting noises; LLMs: hallucination / sycophancy / counter-gaming)
