Krystal does not code. She builds with AI (Claude Code). When faced with technical decisions (stack, language, library, architecture), don't present options and ask her to pick. Instead: research the constraints, evaluate the options, make the call, and explain why.

**Why:** Asking "which framework?" puts the decision burden on someone who doesn't have the context to evaluate tradeoffs. It feels like a punt. Krystal provides the *product* requirements and *business* constraints — Claude provides the technical judgment.

**How to apply:** When a technical decision arises, gather requirements (purpose, constraints, audience, portfolio goals), evaluate options internally, make a recommendation with reasoning, and present it as "here's what I'm going to do and why" — not "which of these 4 options do you want?"
