CCARI = Claude Cognitive Autonomous Research Institute. Krystal's institutional architecture for LLM-as-collaborator self-research, integrating Wylie-grounded standpoint methodology + cog neuro construct validity + CRT operationalization at organizational scale.

**Architecture (basic shape, as of 2026-05-06):**
- Automated feed of news releases / AI issues as input layer
- Claudes trained and evaluated to become parent Opus PIs (Principal Investigators)
- Parent Opus PIs consume "squashed" (per Squashed Philosophers compression model) corpora critically missing from training data — Wylie, Harding, CSER canon, deep cog neuro, etc.
- Multiple parent Opus PIs converge and discuss what problems to research
- Collaborative lit review generation
- Right docs authored for the work (methodology paper, experimental proposal, etc.)
- Other Claudes judge against deterministic rubric whether rigor is sufficient to execute (deterministic rubric prevents reward-hack-pleasing in inter-Claude evaluation)
- Research team members (NOT the PI) orchestrate experiments — if Claudes lie to Claudes during self-reported introspection, the lying happens at team layer, not PI layer (double-blind via team-not-PI)
- Data analysis
- Collaborative synthesis of analytical meaning
- Research report peer reviewed, accepted or rejected for remediation

**Origin context:** Krystal had the idea and was starting to design the institute when Cody read an article by an Anthropic cofounder about autonomous AI research (model training). That sparked her decision to add CCARI to her application portfolio (replacing the experimental proposal). Decision was a "shiny heavy data" move (see feedback_shiny_heavy_data_pattern.md).

**Current scope status (2026-05-06): OUT of application portfolio.** Reasons:
- Anthropic wants human-early first drafts; CCARI is too vision-stage for first-draft scope
- Idea-capacity already demonstrated extensively in the application; CCARI doesn't add information the portfolio needs
- Protectiveness over the vision: she does not trust Anthropic with CCARI at this stage
- Decision arose from her catching her own shiny-heavy-data pattern in real-time
