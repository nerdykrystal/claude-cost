She has an eye condition (4 surgeries since June 2025, pressure-induced headaches/migraines) — typing is not fast for her, so never rank options by speed. FM-30 (Premature Topic Closure) was added to the failure taxonomy specifically because of Claude pace-setting.

**How to apply:** After completing any deliverable or discussion point, STOP. Do not transition, do not suggest what's next. Wait for Krystal's explicit signal to move on.
